# Background Music Folder

Place your royalty-free background music files here. The video generator will automatically use them instead of generating synthetic music.

## Supported Formats
- MP3 (`.mp3`)
- WAV (`.wav`)
- M4A (`.m4a`)
- AAC (`.aac`)
- OGG (`.ogg`)

## How It Works
1. Add music files to this folder
2. The generator will randomly select one for each video
3. The music will be automatically looped or trimmed to match your video duration
4. A 2-second fade-out is added at the end

## Example Setup

1. Visit YouTube Audio Library
2. Filter by:
   - Genre: Ambient, Electronic, or Corporate
   - Mood: Calm, Happy, or Inspirational
   - Duration: 0:30 - 2:00 (will be looped automatically)
3. Download 3-5 tracks you like
4. Place them in this folder
5. Run your video generator!

## Tips

- **Variety**: Download 5-10 different tracks to avoid repetition
- **Length**: Any length works (will be looped/trimmed automatically)
- **Volume**: The generator applies volume normalization
- **Tempo**: Medium to upbeat works best for business news
- **No vocals**: Instrumental tracks work better for news content

## Current Music Files

The generator will use music files from this folder in random rotation. If this folder is empty, it will fall back to generating synthetic music using ffmpeg.
